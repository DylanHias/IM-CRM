# v1.1.0

## New: Analytics page

A new Analytics section is now available in the sidebar. It gives you a visual overview of your work and your customer portfolio across four tabs:

**Personal** — your own activity count (with a comparison to the previous period), follow-up completion rate, open pipeline value, win rate, and a day-by-day activity chart. Use the period picker to switch between 7 days, 30 days, 3 months, 6 months, or 1 year.

**Pipeline** — team-wide pipeline metrics without individual names. Includes a stage funnel showing how many open deals are in each stage, a forecast chart showing weighted vs. total revenue by month, a list of deals expiring in the next 7 or 30 days, and average deal size broken down by sell type and vendor.

**Customers** — health overview of your portfolio. Shows how many active customers have had no activity in 30, 60, or 90 days; contact coverage; cloud adoption rate by segment; ARR distribution across size buckets; ARR totals by industry, segment, or country; and a top-10 customer list by ARR.

**Activity** — patterns across all logged activities. Compares your activity volume to the rest of the team (no individual names shown), breaks down call directions, shows which days of the week are most active, and lists the ten most-engaged customers.
