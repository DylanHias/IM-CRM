- You can now customize your keyboard shortcuts from the new **Keybindings** tab in Settings
- Supports single keys, two-key combos (like Ctrl+K), and three-key combos (like Ctrl+Shift+R or Alt+Shift+N)
- Each shortcut can be reset individually, or you can reset all keybindings at once
- Your custom keybindings are saved and remembered across sessions
- The shortcuts guide and command palette now show your custom keybindings instead of defaults
- The command palette now shows keyboard shortcuts using styled key badges instead of plain text
- The selected item color in the command palette now matches your chosen accent color
