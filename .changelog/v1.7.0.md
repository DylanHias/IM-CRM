v1.7.0

- Added a built-in AI assistant to the app, only visible to admins
- The assistant can answer questions about how the app works and look up customer or contact information
- Ollama (the AI engine) is now bundled with the app and starts automatically in the background - no installation steps needed
- The AI model is downloaded automatically the first time you open the assistant, with a progress bar shown during the download
- The assistant appears as a floating button in the bottom-right corner and opens into a chat panel
