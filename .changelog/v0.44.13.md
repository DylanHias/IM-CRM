# v0.44.13

## First-time user walkthrough

New users now get a guided tour of the app after their initial data sync completes. When you click "Get Started" on the setup screen, the tour automatically walks you through all the main areas:

- **Navigation** — how to move between sections using the sidebar
- **Customers** — how to search, filter, and use favourites
- **Customer detail** — each tab explained: Overview, Activities, Contacts, Follow-Ups, Opportunities
- **Follow-Ups and Opportunities** — your global task and pipeline views
- **Revenue Overview** — your accounts' performance at a glance, including the Excel export
- **Sync** — how your data stays in sync with Dynamics 365

You can skip the tour at any time. It only runs once — subsequent launches go straight to the app.
