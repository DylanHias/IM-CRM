v1.6.0

- Added a floating AI assistant button in the bottom right corner of the app
- Click it to open a chat window where you can ask questions about how the app works
- The assistant knows everything in the help documentation and can answer questions like "how do I add a follow-up?" or "what does the sync page do?"
- You can also ask about specific customers or contacts and it will pull their info from your local data
- Choose which AI model to use from the dropdown in the chat header
- Requires Ollama to be running locally with at least one model downloaded
- Notifications shift left automatically to stay clear of the chat button
