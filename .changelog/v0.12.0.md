# v0.12.0

- Added a new **Admin Panel** with five sections, accessible from a new "Admin" item in the sidebar (only visible to admins)
- **User Management**: See all users who have used the app, their roles, and when they were last active. You can also pull the latest user list from the company directory and promote/demote users to admin
- **Audit Log**: Every time someone creates, edits, or deletes a record (activities, follow-ups, opportunities, contacts), it's now tracked. You can see who changed what, when, and what the old/new values were. You can also pull in changes made by other users from the company system
- **Sync Administration**: See how your data syncs have been performing — success rate, how long they take, and any errors. You can force a fresh sync or clean up old sync history
- **Analytics & Reports**: Charts showing activity breakdown (by type, by month, by user), your opportunity pipeline by stage, win/loss rates, and data quality warnings (like customers with no contacts or no recent activity)
- **Data Management**: See how many records are in each table, export everything to a spreadsheet, and clean up old audit/sync data
- The first user to open the app after this update automatically becomes an admin
