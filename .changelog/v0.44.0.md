# v0.44.0

The sidebar **Opportunities** page has been redesigned to match the look and feel of the Customers page.

- The list is now **paginated** — use the controls at the bottom to move between pages, and pick how many opportunities to show per page (your choice is remembered).
- **Search** by subject, company, or vendor.
- **Sort** by created date, subject, revenue, expiration, or stage.
- **Filter by company** — the dropdown only shows companies that actually have opportunities, so you never scroll through empty options.
- **Filter by stage** to quickly see everything in a specific part of your pipeline.
- Each opportunity row has a **cleaner layout**: a colored icon keyed to the company, the deal subject, the company name, stage, vendor, and a right-side summary showing status, revenue, and expiration at a glance.
- Stale opportunities (open deals with no recent updates) still show the amber warning.
- Hover a row to reveal **edit** and **delete** buttons, just like before.
- Click a row to jump straight to that customer.
