# v0.11.0

- You can now open **Settings** from your account menu at the bottom of the sidebar
- Choose between light, dark, or automatic (system) appearance
- Pick an accent color to personalize the look of the app
- Turn on compact mode to fit more on screen
- Set how many days of silence before a customer is flagged as inactive (was always 90 days, now you choose)
- Pick your preferred default activity type, customer sorting, and export format
- Control whether the app syncs automatically when it launches and how often
- Toggle reminders for overdue follow-ups and stale opportunities
- Your account info (name, sign out, version) has moved from the top bar to the bottom of the sidebar
- The top bar is now cleaner — just your connection status and sync controls
