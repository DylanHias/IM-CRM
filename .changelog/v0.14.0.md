# v0.14.0

- **All settings now actually work.** Previously, most options in Data & Defaults, Notifications, Sync, and Data Management were just for show — changing them did nothing. Now every setting controls real app behavior.
- **Your preferred activity type is remembered.** When logging a new activity, the type you picked in settings (meeting, visit, call, or note) is now pre-selected.
- **Customer list sorts and filters respect your defaults.** The sort order and "show my customers first" toggle now take effect when you open the customer list.
- **Customer list now has pages.** Instead of showing all customers at once, the list is split into pages based on your "items per page" setting. You can flip through pages with simple back/forward buttons.
- **Sidebar remembers your preference.** If you choose "sidebar expanded by default," it starts open when you launch the app.
- **Mock data toggle works.** Turning on "use mock data" in settings now actually loads sample data instead of real synced data.
- **Export format is respected.** The data export button now uses whichever format you picked in settings (Excel or CSV).
- **Auto-sync on launch.** If enabled, the app automatically syncs your data when it starts up — no need to click the sync button.
- **Background sync runs on a timer.** The app now syncs automatically in the background at the interval you set (default: every 15 minutes).
- **Sync notifications.** When a sync finishes, you'll see a small popup in the corner (if you have sync toasts turned on).
- **Overdue follow-up alerts.** When you open the app, you'll be notified if you have overdue follow-ups.
- **Upcoming follow-up reminders.** You'll see a heads-up about follow-ups coming due within the number of days you configured.
- **Stale opportunities are flagged.** Opportunities with no updates beyond your threshold are now marked with a warning icon so you know which ones need attention.
