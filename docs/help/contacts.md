# Contacts

Contacts are the individual people at your customer companies — the stakeholders, decision-makers, and day-to-day contacts you interact with.

## Viewing Contacts

Go to a customer's **Contacts** tab to see all contacts for that company. Each contact shows:

- **Name** (first and last)
- **Job title**
- **Email** and **phone numbers**

Use the search bar to filter contacts by name if the customer has many.

## Adding a Contact

1. Go to a customer's **Contacts** tab
2. Click **Add Contact** or press **N**
3. Fill in the details:
   - **First Name** (required)
   - **Last Name** (required)
   - **Job Title**
   - **Email**
   - **Phone** (office number)
   - **Mobile** (cell number)
   - **Notes** — Internal notes about this contact
4. Click **Save**

## Editing a Contact

Click the **pencil icon** on a contact to update their information.

## Deleting a Contact

Click the **trash icon** and confirm to remove a contact.

## Linking Contacts to Activities

When logging an activity, you can select which contact was involved. This helps you track your interaction history with specific people at a company. When viewing activities, you'll see which contact was linked to each one.

## Sync

Contacts are synced two ways with Dynamics 365:

- **Pull** — contacts from D365 appear automatically after each sync
- **Push** — contacts you add or edit are sent to D365 immediately (or on the next sync if you're offline)
- **Delete** — removing a contact also deletes it from D365
