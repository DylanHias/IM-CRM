# Timeline

The Timeline gives you a single chronological view of all activities, follow-ups, and opportunities across every customer — without having to open each one individually.

## What's in the Timeline

- **Activities** — meetings, calls, visits, and notes you've logged
- **Follow-ups** — tasks and next actions, showing whether they're open or done
- **Opportunities** — deals with their stage, primary vendor, and estimated revenue

Each event shows the customer it belongs to. Click the customer name to jump directly to their detail page.

## Filtering

Use the search bar at the top to filter by subject or customer name. The timeline updates as you type.

## Navigation

You can open the Timeline from:

- The sidebar (Timeline entry)
- The command palette (Ctrl+K → "Go to Timeline")
- Keyboard shortcut (if Timeline is in your sidebar order, press its assigned number)
