# Dashboard

The Dashboard is your personal overview and the first thing you see when you open the app. It gives you a snapshot of what matters right now, without having to dig through different sections. You can change which page opens on launch in **Settings → Appearance → Sidebar & navigation → Default landing tab**.

## At the top

**Greeting** — A friendly welcome with your name and today's date.

**Quick add** — A button in the top-right corner. It opens a short menu:
- **Log activity** — pick a customer, then jump straight to the new-activity form.
- **New follow-up** — pick a customer, then jump straight to the new-follow-up form.
- **New opportunity** — go straight to creating a new opportunity.

**Search everything** — A full-width search bar that searches across customers, contacts, opportunities, activities, and follow-ups at once. Type to see instant results grouped by category. Use arrow keys to move through results and Enter to open. Press `/` from anywhere on the page to jump straight to it.

## The main row

**Belgium map** — Shows a dot for every city where you have customers. The bigger the dot, the more customers there. Hover a dot for the exact number, or click a city to open the Customers list already filtered to it.

**Metric cards** — Four at-a-glance numbers:
- **Total Activities** — every activity you've logged, with the change compared to the last 7 days.
- **Opportunities** — your total number of opportunities, with the 7-day change.
- **Open Pipeline** — the total value of your open deals (in euro), with the change versus 7 days ago.
- **My Customers** — how many accounts you manage.

**Recent Activity** — Your latest 15 activities and opportunity updates, newest first. Each row shows the type, subject, customer, a status badge, and the date. Click any row to open that customer's Activities or Opportunities tab.

**Stale Opportunities** — Your open deals that haven't been touched in 30 days or more, each with a "days stale" badge and its value. Click one to open it and follow up. (Deals left for a very long time even grow a few cobwebs — a friendly nudge.)

## Next 7 Days

A strip of seven day-columns starting with today (today is highlighted). Each column lists the follow-ups you still need to complete on that day — up to three, then a "+N more" note. Each item shows the follow-up title and which customer it belongs to; click it to jump to that customer's Follow-Ups tab. The number in the strip's header is the total follow-ups due across the whole week, and empty days simply show a dash.

## Tips

- The Dashboard is your default landing page unless you change it in Settings.
- Use **Search everything** to jump to any customer, contact, opportunity, activity, or follow-up without navigating through separate sections.
- The **Next 7 Days** strip and **Stale Opportunities** panel together show you what needs attention now and what's slipping — a quick daily check keeps nothing from falling through the cracks.
