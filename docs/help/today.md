# Dashboard

The Dashboard is your personal overview and the first thing you see when you open the app. It gives you a snapshot of what matters right now, without having to dig through different sections.

## What you see

**Greeting** — A friendly welcome message at the top, along with today's date.

**Search bar** — A full-width search bar that searches across customers, contacts, opportunities, and activities at once. Type to see instant results grouped by category. Use arrow keys to navigate results and Enter to open. Press `/` from anywhere on the page to jump straight to the search bar.

**Metric cards** — Three at-a-glance numbers:
- Follow-ups due today (your own, not completed)
- Activities you logged in the last 7 days
- Total pipeline value across all open opportunities

## Cards

**Due Today** — Lists all your incomplete follow-ups with a due date of today. Click the circle next to any item to mark it complete right from this screen. Click the follow-up title to navigate to that customer's follow-ups tab.

**Recent Activities** — The 10 most recent activities logged in the system, regardless of customer. Useful for picking up where you left off. Click any row to go to that customer's activity list.

**Open Opportunities** — All opportunities with status Open, sorted by estimated revenue (highest first). Click any row to open that opportunity.

## Tips

- The Dashboard is your default landing page when you open the app.
- Use the search bar to jump to any customer, contact, opportunity, or activity without navigating through separate sections.
- Completing a follow-up from the Due Today card removes it from the list immediately.
