# Activities

Activities are records of your interactions with customers — meetings, calls, visits, and notes.

## Activity Types

There are four types of activities you can log:

- **Meeting** — A scheduled meeting (in-person or virtual)
- **Visit** — A physical site visit to the customer
- **Call** — A phone conversation
- **Note** — An internal note or update (not a direct interaction)

Each type is shown with a distinct color so you can quickly scan the timeline.

## Logging a New Activity

1. Go to a customer's **Activities** tab
2. Click **Log Activity** or press **N**
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Type** — Select Meeting, Visit, Call, or Note
   - **Subject** — A short summary of what happened (required)
   - **Description** — Detailed notes, outcomes, or next steps
   - **Date/Time** — For Meetings and Visits, set a start and end time. For Calls and Notes, set just the date
   - **Contact** — Optionally link the activity to a specific contact at the company
4. Click **Log Activity** to save

Your default activity type can be set in **Settings > Data & Defaults** so you don't have to select it every time.

## Viewing Activities

Activities appear in a timeline on the customer's Activities tab, with the most recent at the top. Each activity shows:

- The activity type icon and color
- Subject line
- A preview of the description
- Date and time
- The related contact (if linked)
- Who created the activity

## Editing an Activity

Click the **pencil icon** on any activity to edit it. You can change the subject, description, type, date/time, and linked contact.

## Deleting an Activity

Click the **trash icon** on an activity. You'll be asked to confirm before it's deleted.

## Kanban Board

Meetings, visits, and calls are displayed in a **kanban board** with four columns:

- **Open** — Scheduled or in-progress activities
- **Completed** — Activities that have been marked done
- **Rejected** — Cancelled or declined activities
- **Expired** — Activities that passed without being completed

You can **drag and drop** cards between columns to change their status. Empty columns collapse automatically — click the column header to expand or collapse them manually. Each column header shows the item count.

**Notes** appear separately above the kanban board in a list view.

Use the **date filter** (top right of the Activities section) to narrow down which activities are shown by time range.

## Sync Status

Activities sync to Dynamics 365 automatically. You'll see a small badge on activities that are:

- **Pending sync** — Waiting to be sent to D365
- **Sync error** — Failed to sync (will retry on next sync)

Check the **Sync** page for more details on sync status.
