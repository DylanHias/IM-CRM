# Analytics

The Analytics page gives you a visual overview of your work and your portfolio's health — all based on data that's already synced to your device.

## Personal

Shows your own performance for a selected time period (last 7 days, 30 days, 3 months, 6 months, or 1 year).

- **My Activities** — how many activities you logged, with a comparison to the previous period.
- **Follow-Up Completion** — how many of your follow-ups were completed, and how long they took on average.
- **Open Pipeline** — total estimated value of your open opportunities.
- **Win Rate** — percentage of your closed opportunities that were won.
- **Activity Timeline** — a day-by-day chart of your activity volume.

Use the period picker in the top-right to change the time window.

## Pipeline

Shows the overall team pipeline and deal forecast. No individual names are shown here.

- **Stage Funnel** — how many open deals are in each stage, from Prospecting to Purchased.
- **Forecast by Month** — total and probability-weighted revenue from open deals, grouped by their expiration date.
- **Expiring Soon** — open deals closing in the next 7 or 30 days.
- **By Sell Type / By Vendor** — average deal size broken down by sell type and primary vendor.

## Customers

A health overview of your customer portfolio.

- **No Recent Activity** — counts of active customers with no logged activity in the last 30, 60, or 90 days.
- **Contact Coverage** — how many customers have at least one contact recorded.
- **Cloud Adoption** — percentage of customers flagged as cloud customers, broken down by segment.
- **ARR Distribution** — how customers are spread across ARR ranges (No ARR, < €10k, €10k–50k, €50k–200k, €200k+).
- **ARR by Dimension** — total ARR grouped by industry, segment, or country. Use the dropdown to switch.
- **Top 10 by ARR** — your ten highest-ARR active customers.

## Activity

Patterns across all logged activities for the selected period.

- **Activity Type Mix** — a comparison of your own activities vs the rest of the team (no individual names, just totals).
- **Call Direction** — breakdown of logged calls into Outgoing, Incoming, and Unknown.
- **Activity by Day of Week** — when activities are typically logged across the week.
- **Most Active Customers** — the ten customers with the most activity in the selected period.
